Hospital records are stored in `hospital.xml` and validated by `hospital.xsd`.

The XML uses `hospital` as the root element and contains 3 patients. Each patient has:
- unique integer `id`
- name, gender, date of birth, and phone
- at least 2 medical records

Each record includes:
- visit date
- doctor name
- diagnosis
- treatment
- one or more medicines
- billing details

The XSD validates:
- `id` as integer
- `gender` as Male or Female
- dates using `xs:date`
- phone number with exactly 10 digits
- costs as decimal values
- unique patient IDs

This helps keep the hospital data valid, consistent, and well-structured.
